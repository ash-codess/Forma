Process

How FORMA
works.

A transparent, four-step process — entirely within your browser, no external dependencies for your data.

01

Select Your Files

Drag and drop images or PDFs directly onto the tool. Multiple files are supported for batch operations.

02

Configure Settings

Set dimensions, quality, format, and other parameters. All settings update the output in real time.

03

Browser Processes

Your browser's Canvas API and WebAssembly engine handle all transformations locally at native speed.

04

Instant Download

Results download directly from memory. Multiple outputs are auto-zipped. No account required.
